Telephone Inquiries

  1. Ask patient to verify Name and DOB
  2. Ask patient how you may help them
  3. Start Phone Note in EMR, document patients words
  4. Determine if you can assist patient or if MD needs to review
  5. Notifiy patient of estimated time for reply to their request

Follow up:

Make sure to follow up with the patient as soon as MD has responded or by end of day, notify if further action is needed.

Medication Refills

  1. Ask patient to verify Name and DOB
  2. Ask patient for name of medication(s)
  3. Start Med Refill in EMR, document patients words
  4. Review patient's chart for last fill date/last visit/last labs if needed then send to MD to review
  5. Notifiy patient of estimated time for reply to their request

Follow up:

Make sure to follow up with the patient if any appointments or labs are needed.

Rooming Patients

  1. Ask patient to verify Name and DOB
  2. Record W/H/BP/P/R, O2 if SOB/cough/sick
  3. Review in EMR Med list/Med History/Allegeries
  4. Document reason for visit/symptoms/how long
  5. Inform MD patient ready

Follow up:

Make sure to give patient Summary after visit and schedule any follow ups.

Prior Authorizations

  1. Verify Name and DOB prior to starting paperwork
  2. Verify Medication Name, dose, directions
  3. Review patients chart notes for documentation for PA form
  4. Fill out form as best as possible, print and attach chart/phone notes supporting medication
  5. Place in MD in box for review and signature, then fax when completed

Follow up:

Make sure to follow up with the patient once Auth had been reviewed by MD notify patient of approval or denial and next steps.

Scheduling Imaging

  1. Verify Name and DOB prior to starting paperwork
  2. Ask patient best day/time, call Imaging
  3. Verify if PA is needed or not
  4. Fill out form print and attach chart notes for supporting imaging order
  5. Fax completed paperwork

Follow up:

Make sure to follow up with the patient the results once MD has reviewed report.

Vaccination Process

  1. Receive order from MD
  2. Verify medication/vaccine name, dose, directions
  3. Draw up vaccine/medication
  4. Pull fact shee if needed
  5. Verify patient name/dob, use proper PPE and hand sanitizing technique, administer medication/vaccine, then document in EMR including location given

Follow up:

Make sure to follow up with the patient by end of day or within 24 hours to see if they experienced any side affects.
